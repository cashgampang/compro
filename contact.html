Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What makes your milk different from others?

Our milk comes from grass-fed cows on local farms, ensuring the highest quality and freshness. We use no artificial additives and maintain strict quality control throughout our production process.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes! We offer daily home delivery service within Jakarta and surrounding areas. Orders placed before 6 PM will be delivered the next morning between 6-9 AM.

How long does the milk stay fresh?

Our milk stays fresh for 5-7 days when properly refrigerated. We recommend consuming within 3 days of opening for the best taste and quality.

Can I customize my order?

You can mix and match different types of milk, adjust delivery frequency, and even pause deliveries when you're away. Contact our customer service for personalized orders.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and popular e-wallet services like GoPay, OVO, and DANA for your convenience.
